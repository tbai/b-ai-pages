Getting Started

How do I create my first coloring page?
  1. Open the app and make sure you're on the "Words" tab
  2. Tap emoji category buttons to see words (Animals, Actions, Adjectives)
  3. Tap on words you like to select them
  4. When you have 1-3 words selected, tap "Generate Drawing"
  5. Wait a few seconds while AI creates your unique drawing
  6. Your drawing is automatically saved to the Gallery!

Features

What's the difference between Words and Text modes?

Words Mode (Free): Choose from pre-selected, kid-friendly words organized by emoji categories.

Text Mode (Premium): Type anything you want (up to 100 characters). Perfect for custom ideas like "unicorn flying over rainbow castle."

How do I print my drawings?
  1. Go to the Gallery tab
  2. Tap on the drawing you want to print
  3. Tap the Print button (printer icon)
  4. Select your printer and print!

All drawings are optimized with black lines on white background, perfect for any home printer.

Can I share my drawings?

Sharing is a premium feature. With the premium upgrade, tap the Share button on any drawing to send it via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or save it to Photos.

How many drawings can I save?

Unlimited! Save as many drawings as you want. All drawings are stored on your device. You can delete any drawing by swiping left or tapping the Delete button.

Troubleshooting

The app says my device is not supported. Why?

Print and Paint requires:

  • iOS 26.0 or later
  • iPhone or iPad with Apple Intelligence support
  • Compatible devices: iPhone 15 Pro or later, iPad with M1 chip or later

The app uses Apple's ImagePlayground API which requires these newer devices.

Generation is taking a long time or failing

Try these steps:

  1. Make sure your device has enough free storage (at least 1GB recommended)
  2. Close other apps running in the background
  3. Restart the app
  4. If it continues, restart your device
  5. Check that you're running iOS 26.0 or later

Note: First generation after app launch may take longer as the AI model loads.

My drawings look different than I expected

AI generation is creative and unpredictable! Each drawing is unique. If you don't like a result:

  • Try different word combinations
  • Generate multiple times with the same words - you'll get different results
  • With premium text mode, be more specific in your description
  • Keep the combinations simple for best results
The drawing has gray areas instead of pure black and white

This shouldn't happen - all drawings are processed to be pure black lines on white background. If you see gray areas:

  1. Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  2. Try generating the drawing again
  3. If it persists, contact support with a screenshot
I can't find the Gallery tab

The Gallery is at the bottom of the screen:

  • iPhone: Tap the "Gallery" tab at the bottom
  • iPad: Look for the Gallery in the sidebar or navigation

If you don't see it, make sure you're running the latest version of the app.

Premium purchase isn't working

If you purchased premium but it's not unlocked:

  1. Tap "Restore Purchases" on the premium screen
  2. Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
  3. Check your purchase history in App Store settings
  4. Restart the app
  5. If still not working, contact Apple Support for purchase issues

Technical Information

What are the system requirements?
  • iOS Version: iOS 26.0 or later
  • Compatible iPhones: iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, iPhone 16 series
  • Compatible iPads: iPad Pro (M1 or later), iPad Air (M1 or later)
  • Storage: ~50MB for app, plus space for saved drawings
  • Internet: Required for initial download, then works offline
Does this work offline?

Yes! After you download the app, everything works completely offline. The AI runs on your device, so you can generate drawings anywhere - on a plane, in a car, or anywhere without internet.

How does the AI work?

Print and Paint uses Apple's ImagePlayground framework, which runs entirely on your device. The AI model generates unique sketches based on your word selections, then the app processes them into clean black and white line art perfect for coloring.

Tips & Tricks

How do I get the best results?
  • Start with 2-3 words for good combinations
  • Mix categories - try an animal + action + adjective
  • Keep it simple for younger kids
  • Generate multiple times if you want variations
  • With premium text mode, be descriptive but concise
  • Remember: each generation is unique, even with the same words!
Can I print on different paper sizes?

Yes! When you tap Print, your device's print dialog lets you choose paper size, orientation, and other print settings. The drawings scale to fit your chosen size.
