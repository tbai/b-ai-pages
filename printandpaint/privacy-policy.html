Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2024

Simple Summary: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything happens on your device.

Introduction

Print and Paint ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

None. We do not collect any personal information or usage data.

Print and Paint is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • We don't collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't collect device identifiers
  • We don't track which drawings you create
  • We don't monitor how you use the app
  • We don't use analytics or tracking services
  • We don't have user accounts or logins

How the App Works

Print and Paint uses Apple's on-device ImagePlayground API to generate coloring pages:

  • All image generation happens entirely on your device
  • Your word selections never leave your device
  • Generated drawings are stored locally in your device's database
  • No data is sent to our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • The app works completely offline after download

Data Storage

All data created by Print and Paint is stored locally on your device:

  • Generated drawings are saved using SwiftData (Apple's local database)
  • Your gallery of drawings stays on your device
  • Premium purchase status is managed by Apple through StoreKit
  • If you delete the app, all your drawings are permanently deleted

Third-Party Services

Apple ImagePlayground

Print and Paint uses Apple's ImagePlayground framework to generate drawings. This processing happens entirely on your device. Apple does not collect information about the prompts or images generated. For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple In-App Purchases

If you purchase the premium upgrade, the transaction is handled by Apple through their StoreKit framework. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple manages purchase verification and Family Sharing. See Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Print and Paint is designed for children and families. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect any personal information from children
  • We do not use any tracking or analytics
  • The app contains no advertisements
  • There are no social features or user-to-user communication
  • All content is age-appropriate and family-friendly

Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to:

  • Access or download
  • Correct or update
  • Delete or remove
  • Export or transfer

You have complete control over your drawings:

  • Delete individual drawings from your gallery anytime
  • Deleting the app removes all drawings permanently

Data Security

While we don't collect data, the data you create is protected:

  • Drawings are stored in your device's secure database
  • Data is protected by iOS security features
  • No transmission of data to external servers
  • Premium status is verified through Apple's secure StoreKit

International Users

Print and Paint is available worldwide. Since all processing happens on your device and we don't collect any data, there are no data transfer or international privacy concerns.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. However, since Print and Paint does not collect, store, or share any personal information, these rights do not apply as there is no personal information to access, delete, or manage.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, Print and Paint complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect any personal data, GDPR data subject rights regarding access, rectification, erasure, and portability do not apply.
