Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

b/AI requires iOS 26.0 or later and a device with Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) or A17 Pro chip. This includes:

  • iPhone 16 and newer
  • iPad with M-series chips
  • Devices with A17 Pro or newer

Does b/AI require an internet connection?

No! b/AI works completely offline. All AI processing happens on your device using Apple's on-device Foundation Models. You can use it on flights, in remote areas, or anywhere without internet.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your conversations never leave your device. We don't have servers, we don't collect data, and we can't access your chats. Everything is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

Is b/AI really free?

Yes! b/AI is free to download and use. No subscriptions, no monthly fees, and no ads.

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, simply delete the app. There are no cloud backups or server-side storage.

Can I export my conversations?

Currently, conversations are stored locally on your device. You can copy individual messages by long-pressing on them in the chat interface.

Why does my device need Apple Silicon or A17 Pro?

b/AI uses Apple's Foundation Models framework, which requires advanced neural processing capabilities. These are only available on newer devices with Apple Silicon (M-series) or A17 Pro chips.

I'm having technical issues. What should I do?

Please email tixbai@icloud.com with:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • A description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable)
  • Any screenshots that might help
