About the App

Papo de Anjo (Angel Chat) is a children's storybook app inspired by the teachings of "The Gospel According to Spiritism" by Allan Kardec, adapted for reading moments between parents and children.

How to Use

1. Open the app and tap "Sortear História" (Draw Story)

2. Read the story to your child

3. Swipe left or right to see other stories

FAQ

Does the app need internet?
No. All content is available offline.

How do I clear the reading history?
Uninstall and reinstall the app.
