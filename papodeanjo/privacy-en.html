Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Papo de Anjo (Angel Chat) is an app designed to provide spiritual reading moments between parents and children. This policy describes how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Papo de Anjo was built with privacy as a priority. The app:

  • Does not collect personal data - We do not ask for your name, email, age, or any personal information.
  • Does not require registration - The app works without creating an account.
  • Does not use the internet - All content is available offline.
  • Does not track location - We do not access your location.

2. Data Stored Locally

The app stores only on your device:

  • A record of which stories have been read, to avoid repetition.

This data remains only on your device and is not sent to any server.

3. Children's Privacy

Papo de Anjo is intended to be used by parents with their children. The app:

  • Does not collect any information from children.
  • Contains no advertising.
  • Has no external links or social media.
  • Does not allow communication with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect data, there is no data to share with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

6. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act)

In compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected (no personal data is collected).
  • Request deletion of data (local data can be deleted by uninstalling the app).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through an app update.
